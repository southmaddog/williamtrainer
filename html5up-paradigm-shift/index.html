About Me

I am William Ye, a currently level 4 years Kinesiology student, and a certified personal trainer. Honestly, I have more experienced of being an athelete than a trainer. During my years of shool, I have been invloving in High school track and wrestling team to compete. Until university I finally settle in the JiuJi-stu team in McMaster now. I am not muscle hypertrophy specialized Due to the sport I am playing, I have to control my weight to compete in certain weight class. However, I have a lot of experience of boosting sport performance and change body composition.

  • Boost Sport Performance
  • Changing Body composition
  • Posture correction

  • Increase Range of Motion

My approach aims at achieving optimal athletic results while maintaining the necessary weight for competitive success."
